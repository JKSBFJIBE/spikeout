RYŪNOSUKE TANAKA

Date of Birth : March 3, 1996

Height : 177.2 cm (5' 9.8") - Apr 2012 178.2 cm (5' 10.2") - Nov 2012

Teams : Oujitsu Junior High (Former) Karasuno High

Number : 9 (High School 1st Year) 5 (High School 2nd Year)

Position : Outside Hitter , Ace (3rd Year)

Ryūnosuke Tanaka (Japanese: 田中たなか 龍之介りゅうのすけ Tanaka Ryūnosuke) for the majority of the series is a second-year student at Karasuno High. He is part of the Boys' Volleyball Club and plays as a starting wing spiker. Tanaka was known as the team's up-and-coming ace. After graduating high school, he became a personal trainer and married Kiyoko Shimizu.
